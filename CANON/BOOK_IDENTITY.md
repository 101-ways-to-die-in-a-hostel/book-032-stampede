# Book Identity

## Core Information

**Book Number:** 032
**Title:** Crush Hour
**Slug:** stampede
**Category:** Fellow Travelers

## The Death (One Sentence)
A social media-obsessed traveler is trampled to death when a false fire alarm triggers a stampede in an overcrowded hostel common room during a World Cup screening.

## Tagline
"When the crowd moves, you move. Or you don't move again."

## Themes
1. Mob mentality and herd behavior - satirizes how quickly civilized travelers become a survival-minded mass
2. The dangers of overcrowding for profit
3. Social media's role in creating deadly gatherings (and documenting them)

## Tone Balance
- Comedy: 30%
- Horror: 45%
- Satire: 25%

## Target Reader
Anyone who has been in an overcrowded hostel event, travelers who choose hostels for the social scene, those who have experienced claustrophobic crowd situations.

## Comparison Titles
- The Station nightclub fire documentary meets Hostel - crowd crush horror in backpacker setting

## Content Warnings
- Crowd crush/trampling death
- Claustrophobic situations
- Mass panic
- Graphic injury descriptions
